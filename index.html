About Me

I worked for one year at Eton Bioscience as a lab technician where I processed DNA samples and performed weekly and monthly sequencer maintenance. I am currently in the Masters of Microbial Biotechnology program at North Carolina State University. At NC State I work as a teaching assistant for introduction to biology lab. I am also currently in the Web Development boot-camp certificate program through the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill.
